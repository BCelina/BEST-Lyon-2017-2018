Hey crazy European Students !

In this course you'll find the best way EVER to spend your holidays ! The awesome organizers from Local BEST Group Lyon are preparing for you an unforgettable stay.
If you wish you could discover the amazing city of Lyon and enjoy our wonderful landscapes and buildings here in France ...
If you want to see how crazy are the nights over there ...
If you're willing to have a taste of French gastronomy ...
If you're interested in french kisses ... (and more if affinity)
If you'd like to drink some REAL good wine ...
If you wanna check out some french (good !) clichés ...
If you focus on meeting new friends ...
If you want to know how to achieve sustainibility in this crazy world and how to make the world greener ... Don’t hesitate anymore ! Apply now to our Spring Course “God save the green !” and come from 9th to 18th april 2016.
We are looking forward to see you and have fun with you !

Thanks to the SpC coreteam, and all the LBG Lyon
